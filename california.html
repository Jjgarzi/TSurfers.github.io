Our Favorite California Beaches

Lechuza Beach

Relatively secluded area yet with pedestrian access. You'll find breathtaking rock formations covered with life.

Dana Point

Dana Point is situated between San Diego and Los Angeles, within Orange County. On Weekends,you'll find volunteers that are able to guide you in guided tours.

Leo Carrillo State Park

This spot has opportunities for beachgoers in Malibu. Wether it's fun in the shore, camping or tidepool observing.

Crystal Cove State Park

Nezzled between Newport Beach and Laguna Beach. Crystal Cove makes a lasting impression with a bit of nostalgia as well as historic vistas.

La Jolla Caves Beach

Located near the Sand Diego coast. A short hike to experience a expectacular view. Caves are accesible when there's a low tide. Close encounters with sea life as well as other individuals dving or kayaking.

Terramar Beach

Nicknamed the Carlsbad Tidepools this beach in southern Carlsbad Ca. is known for its local surfing sceane. This particlaur beach transforms into a tipepooling great for the whole family.
