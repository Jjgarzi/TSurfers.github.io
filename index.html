Our Voyage

We started this voyage of traveling along the coast about a year ago. Although we started as strangers, we began to bond for our love of exploration. For us, low tides revealed a multitude of tide pools became mysterious windows into wondrous galaxies right at our fingertips. We began to call ourselves Tide Surfers , always waiting for the next tide to recede.
